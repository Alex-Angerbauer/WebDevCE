Alex 3 Month Events

December

December 24 - Christmas Eve

Christmas Eve is the day before Christmas. A.K.A the best holiday ever.
On Christmas Eve, people gather together as familes and friends and prepare for Christmas day.

December 25 - Christmas

On Christmas, people who participate in the Christian religion celebrate the birth of their savior Jesus Christ.
Along with this, every kid gets toys and presents in the morning.
People also eat very good food on this day while they gather with their family.

April

April 10 - Family Ski Day

On this day, every family in Heber decides to skip school and work so they can go skiing with their family that day.
This is an amazing family experience even though the resorts get very crowded.

April 19 - Easter Day

On Easter Day, people celebrate the resurrection of Jesus Christ. this is again a holiday that Christians celebrate.
On this day people hide eggs and look for them. It is kind of a waste of food, but whatever!

May

May 4 - May the 4th Be With You

On this day people dress up in Star Wars apparel.

May 15 - Schools Out... Sort of

This is when teachers stop giving out assignements.
However the kids still have to go to school because it is required by law.
