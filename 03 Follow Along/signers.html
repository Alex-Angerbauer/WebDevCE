Who were the signers?

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Ah, the quill of liberty itself! This most learned gentleman didst pen the Declaration’s sacred words, and anon rose to rule as the third sovereign—nay, President—of these United States.
John Adams
A fiery spirit of law and order, Master Adams did argue with valiant passion for independence. In time, he ascended to the second highest seat in the land.
Benjamin Franklin
Wise as the ancient owls of Athens, Franklin, a man of letters and lightnings, did lend his wit and wisdom to the cause of freedom and foreign alliance.
John Hancock
Behold! The mightiest signature upon the scroll! Lord Hancock, presider of the Continental Congress, didst write his name large, that even the blind King George might see it.
Samuel Adams
A brewer of both ale and rebellion, this cousin of John Adams didst stir the hearts of men with righteous indignation and call for the crown’s chains to be cast off.
